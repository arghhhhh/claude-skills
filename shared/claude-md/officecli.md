## OfficeCLI - Office Document Creation & Editing

When user wants to create, read, analyze, or modify Office documents (.docx, .xlsx, .pptx), read `~/.claude/skills/officecli/SKILL.md`.

For specialized workflows, the core skill references dedicated skills: `officecli-docx`, `officecli-pptx`, `officecli-xlsx`, `officecli-pitch-deck`, `officecli-academic-paper`, `officecli-data-dashboard`, `officecli-financial-model`, `morph-ppt`.
